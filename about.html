About

We have all seen people who are experiencing homelessness or poverty and think that we have some level of understanding of them.
But we tend to think of ourselves as “WE,” and homeless people as “THEM.”

Regardless of the level of exposure, understanding, and empathy, some may still dissociate, neglect, and excuse oneself from those who are in less fortunate circumstances.

,But Not Because is a project centred around “THEM”

Essentially, we are they and they are we.

Goals

“,But Not Because” is a project constructed by series of conversations, showcasing individuals' stories to the audience at UBC. Through listening and sharing, we hope to build a stronger communication and understanding between communities.

The project attempts to

  • Break down stigma and disassociation towards people who are experiencing, or who have experienced poverty or homelessness

  • Encourage communication and prompt meaningful interactions between communities.
